    • eBookshelves FAQs

    What formats of ebooks does eBookshelves support?

    For now, eBookshelves only supports ebooks in PDF formats. However, in the future, eBookshelves will supports a wide range of ebook formats, including EPUB, MOBI, and more.

    Can I access my ebooks offline?

    Yes, eBookshelves allows you to access your ebooks even when you are offline. Once you have downloaded your ebooks to your device, you can read them without an internet connection.

    How do I add ebooks to my library?

    You can add ebooks to your library by clicking on 'Find Local eBooks' from your booklist page and uploading them directly to eBookshelves, or by importing them from your device's file system.

    Can I create custom tags for my ebooks?

    For the time being, all your eBooks will be organized in a single booklist, but sure in future release, eBookshelves will allows you to create custom tags for your ebooks, which you can use to organize them in a way that makes sense to you.

    Does eBookshelves have a search function?

    Yes, eBookshelves has a search function that allows you to search for ebooks by title, author, or any other keywords.

    Is my data secure with eBookshelves?

    Yes, eBookshelves takes the security of your data very seriously. All your data is encrypted and stored securely on your device, and we do not share your data with any third parties.

    Does eBookshelves offer any social features?

    No, eBookshelves is primarily a digital library and does not offer any social features at this time.

    Is eBookshelves free?

    Yes, eBookshelves is free to use, but in the future, we may offer a premium version with additional features for a small fee.
